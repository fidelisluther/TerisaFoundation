Supporting Girls and Empowering Education

We are excited to announce our upcoming donation event at St. Mary's, where we will be donating sanitary pads and laptops to support girls' education and empowerment. At Terisa Foundation, we believe that every girl deserves access to education, regardless of their background or financial situation.
During the event, we will be distributing sanitary pads to the girls in attendance, ensuring that they have the necessary supplies to manage their menstrual cycle with dignity. We understand that access to menstrual products is a critical issue for girls in underserved communities, often leading to missed school days and stigmatization.
In addition to the sanitary pads, we will also be donating 33 laptops to the school. These laptops will be used to provide basic computer skills training to the students, including topics such as cyber security, coding, and computer packages. We believe that introducing young girls to technology will help them develop skills that are essential for their future success.
We invite everyone to join us in this noble cause by donating funds, sanitary pads, and laptops to support our initiatives. We are committed to empowering the girls and women of Kenya, and with your help, we can achieve our mission.
Thank you for your support, and we look forward to seeing you at St. Mary's.
